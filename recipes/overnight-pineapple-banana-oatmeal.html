Overnight Pineapple-Banana Oatmeal

Description

Overnight oatmeal flavored with pineapple, banana, and pecans is an awesome way to start the day. It's a healthy, quick-and-easy, nutrient packed, make-ahead breakfast ready when you are.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups old fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 ½ cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 8oz can crushed pineapple in pineapple juice
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon cardamom
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 2 bananas, sliced
  • ¼ cup chopped toasted pecans, or to taste
