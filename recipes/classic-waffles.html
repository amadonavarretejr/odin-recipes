Classic Waffles

Description

Classic waffles are a beloved breakfast delight, featuring a delightful balance of crispy exterior and fluffy interior. Whether enjoyed on their own or adorned with various toppings, such as sweet syrups, fresh fruits, or indulgent whipped cream, classic waffles are a timeless choice for a delightful morning meal or a special brunch gathering.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla.
  4. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  5. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron.
  6. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
